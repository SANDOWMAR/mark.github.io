About Us

We are dedicated to promoting food safety in homes, restaurants, and businesses. Learn the proper techniques for food handling, storage, and hygiene.

Food Safety Guidelines

  • Wash hands and surfaces often.
  • Separate raw meat from other foods.
  • Cook to the right temperature.
  • Refrigerate foods promptly.

Helpful Tips

Always check expiration dates, thaw foods safely, and avoid cross-contamination. Use food thermometers to ensure safe internal temperatures.
